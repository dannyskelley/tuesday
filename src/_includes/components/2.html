Dear members and friends of Tuesday Musicale

We’ve got a very interesting and unusual concert coming up on Tuesday December 10. The concert will be at Lutheran Church of the Redeemer, 1800 W. Maple in Birmingham 10:30 a.m.

Never before have we presented “Boogie Woogie”, but we will be showcasing Matthew Ball, a mesmerizing exemplar of the art.

Along a completely different line will be audience participation in the singing of Christmas carols. Also on the program will be a piano duo and an instrumental ensemble.

The concert will be followed by lunch, for which you will need to make a reservation. You can reserve by clicking into the orange oval on the home page of this website or by calling 313-520-8663. Deadline for reservations: Thursday December 5.

I look forward to seeing you on Tuesday December 10.

Regards
Eric Winter
President
