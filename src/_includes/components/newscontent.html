    November 12, 2024 Tuesday Musicale Program and Business Meeting

    On Tuesday morning, November 12, we will be presenting a concert at Westminster Presbyterian Church in Detroit. Members will be performing on a wide variety of instruments ranging from organ to oboe. We will be presenting an original composition for six instruments by Tuesday Musicale composer Colin Payne.

    Dalos Grobe will perform on the outstandingly fine organ at Westminster Church. Violinist Joseph Deller and his string ensemble will perform, as well as Student Leaguer violinist Milan Forrester.

    Westminster Presbyterian Church is located on Hubbell Street at Outer Drive in Detroit.

    10:30 AM Westminster Presbyterian Church
    17567 Hubbell St. (at Outer Drive) Detroit, MI 48235

    Chair of the Day: Eric Winter

    Performers:
    1. Dalos Grobe, Organ
    2. Colin Payne, Composer
    3. Joseph Deller, Violin and String Ensemble
    4. Student Leaguer: Milan Forrester, Violin Piano Collaborator: TBA
